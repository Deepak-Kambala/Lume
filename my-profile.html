About

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a leading expert in Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on ethical AI development and its societal impact. She leads the Responsible AI Lab at MIT, conducting groundbreaking research in fair machine learning, transparency, and accountability in AI systems. Her work aims to build AI technologies that are robust, equitable, and beneficial for humanity. She is a strong advocate for interdisciplinary collaboration and frequently publishes in top-tier conferences and journals.

**Research Interests:** Ethical AI, Fair Machine Learning, AI Transparency, Explainable AI (XAI), Human-AI Interaction, Data Privacy, Societal Impact of AI.
